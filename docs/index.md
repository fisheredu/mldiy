\rightarrow
\text{small experiments}
\rightarrow
\text{proof}
\rightarrow
\text{full experiments}
$$

My 6 questions for myself to read paper actively

1. What exact problem is being solved?
2. Why do previous methods fail?
3. What is the main technical idea?
4. Which assumption is doing the most work?
5. What experiment would make the paper look weak?
6. What happens if one assumption is removed?
